Event Planner

Plan out all of your events here on this calendar

Instructions:

1. Create an event on the calendar by pressing the "Create Event" with the + symbol.

2. Fill out the following template that asks for the name of the event, event date, and event time.

3. Pick out a color that you prefer and press add event, or you can cancel and press on the "X".

4. Hover over the event card to expand and scroll through the details of the event, or delete the event by pressing the "X" on the top right when hovered.

5. You can now view your created event in the calendar below, when you hover over the colored square date, you will be able to see the details of your event that you have created.
